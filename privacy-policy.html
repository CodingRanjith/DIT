1. Introduction

Dharshan India Traders Private Limited (“we”, “us”, “our”, or “DIT”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us for construction materials enquiries and quotes.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Product interest and project / delivery details shared in enquiries
  • Any other information you include in messages, calls, or WhatsApp communication

We do not require account registration to browse this website. Basic technical data (such as browser type or pages visited) may be processed by hosting or analytics tools if enabled in the future.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to quote requests and product enquiries
  • Communicate about orders, delivery, and support
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share details only with trusted service providers who assist our operations (for example, delivery logistics or IT hosting), or when required by law. Any such parties are expected to handle information appropriately.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information shared with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law. Contact us using the details below to make such a request.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party services (for example, Google Maps or WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. Please review their policies separately.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

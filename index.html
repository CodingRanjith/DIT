About DIT

Building Projects with Quality Materials

Dharshan India Traders Private Limited is a leading construction materials trading company, dedicated to providing high-quality products and services to our valued clients. As direct dealers for construction materials, electrical items, plumbing supplies, and steel, we are your one-stop solution for all construction needs.

Incorporated on 17 September 2025 and MCA-registered, we supply builders, contractors, and developers across Pudukkottai and the surrounding Tamil Nadu region.

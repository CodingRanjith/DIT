Our Story

Who We Are

Dharshan India Traders Private Limited is a leading construction materials trading company, dedicated to providing high-quality products and services to our valued clients. As direct dealers for construction materials, electrical items, plumbing supplies, and steel, we are your one-stop solution for all construction needs.

With industry experience and MCA-registered corporate credibility, we supply builders, contractors, and developers across the region. We deliver the right materials on time and within budget, with tailored solutions for large infrastructure projects and small renovations alike.

Incorporated on 17 September 2025 and based in Avudaiyarkoil, Pudukkottai, Tamil Nadu, we operate with full transparency as a Private Limited Company (CIN: U46632TN2025PTC184570).
